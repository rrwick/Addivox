### Videos from others

The resources listed below aren't affiliated with Addivox, but they contain helpful information about software synthesizers and wind controllers:

- [How to use SWAM instruments with your wind synth](https://youtu.be/g0hb61_lpzs) from [iSax.Academy](https://www.youtube.com/@isax.academy): focused on SWAM instruments, but many topics covered (MIDI, buffer size, Bluetooth, etc) also apply to Addivox.
- [Why MainStage is Great for Digital Wind Instruments](https://youtu.be/40_XUlOGEZU) from [Stef Haynes](https://www.youtube.com/@StefHaynes): walks through a MainStage concert setup and shows how MIDI CC can control a synth. Uses Apple's ES1, but many concepts apply to Addivox too.
- [Find Your Voice - Playing Synth Sounds](https://youtu.be/BCMV_FM1Omk), a podcast from [Aerophone Academy](https://www.aerophoneacademy.com/): discusses embracing synth sounds on their own terms, rather than treating them as emulations of acoustic instruments.
